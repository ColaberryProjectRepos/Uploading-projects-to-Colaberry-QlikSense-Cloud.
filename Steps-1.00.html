What is Qlik Sense Cloud? 1
  • Any Qlik Sense project built in  colaberry is later uploaded to the Qlik Sense cloud to be shared with others.
  • The cloud allows users to view the projects and download them.
  • This will help others learn from the previous projects.
  • The next steps will help you take the apps you have created from your computer, to the Colaberry Qlik Sense cloud.
